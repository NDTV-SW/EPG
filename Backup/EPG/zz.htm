How do I check the status of my leave application?
Click on "My availed Leaves" link in the box. You'll get a list of leaves which are sanctioned or are pending for your HOD's approval.

I need to cancel my leave. How do I do it?
Click on "My availed Leaves" link, then click on "Cancel" link against the leave that you want to cancel. If the application is still pending for approval, you'll be able to delete this leave yourself. In case it's a sanctioned leave, your HOD will receive your cancellation request and this leave will get deleted from the system only after the HOD approves the cancellation.

I applied for the same leave twice - resulting in double deduction from my total leaves. What do I do?
Apply for cancellation for one of them.

How do I change the dates for my leave?
Go to "My availed Leaves" page. If the application is still pending for approval, you can cancel this leave yourself and apply afresh with the new dates. In case it's sanctioned, then click on "Change Date" link and your HOD will receive your request.

The HODs specified on my leave page are incorrect.
Please write a mail to HR@ndtv.com saying so. They'll do the required.

I have to apply for medical leave. How do I go about it?
Do keep Dr Nazli informed about your situation. You may need to provide your doctor's prescription for record.
